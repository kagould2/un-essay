Galileo's "Discorsi e Dimostrazioni Matematiche"

"Discorsi e Dimostrazioni Matematiche" (Mathematical Discourses and Demonstrations) was written by the Italian scientist Galileo Galilei, published in 1638. In this book, Galileo presents a comprehensive treatise on mechanics and the principles of motion, expanding upon his earlier work on the laws of nature.
