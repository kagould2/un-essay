Exploring the Encyclopédie

The Encyclopédie, subtitled "ou Dictionnaire raisonné des sciences, des arts et des métiers" (Encyclopedia, or a Systematic Dictionary of the Sciences, Arts, and Crafts), is a monumental work edited by Denis Diderot and Jean le Rond d'Alembert. Published in France between 1751 and 1772, it aimed to compile and disseminate knowledge across various disciplines, including philosophy, science, arts, and crafts.
